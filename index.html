About this page

This podcast started when Zack discovered Evan's first podcast, the PadBros podcast. Evan had a great podcasting voice, but Zack, a non gamer, did not keep up with the game and much of the podcast was wasted on him. He asked Evan if he would do another podcast. They decided to do one about the climbing community in the South Bay (of San Francisco). Evan came up with the name, Zack found the space, and built the website and does most of the editing. If it feels like the podcast is only funny to Zack, that is mostly by design.
